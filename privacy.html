🛡️ Privacy Policy

At ToolNOVA, your privacy is our top priority. We follow a strict "Zero-Retention Policy" to ensure your data is always safe.

1. File Processing :

All files uploaded for resizing, compression, or conversion are processed on our secure temporary servers. Once the processing is complete and you download your file, it is automatically queued for deletion.

2. Data Storage :

We DO NOT store, share, or sell any of your personal images or documents. Our system is designed to clear all temporary data (the /tmp folder) every few minutes.

3. Cookies & Analytics :

We use minimal cookies only to improve your user experience and analyze website traffic. We do not track your personal identity.

4. Third-Party Services :

We use Formspree for newsletter subscriptions. If you subscribe, your email is only used to send updates about new tools. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Your trust is important to us. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
